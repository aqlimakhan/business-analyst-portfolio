## Discussion Summary

Sarah explained that export shipments are experiencing increasing delays due to the current manual export compliance screening process. As export volumes continue to grow, the existing process is becoming difficult to scale. Leadership has requested a Business Analysis initiative to understand the current process and recommend improvements while maintaining regulatory compliance.

The attendees agreed that representatives from Trade Compliance, Logistics, Sales Operations, and IT should participate throughout the project.

The team agreed that the first phase of the project will focus on understanding the current process before discussing any potential solutions.

---

## Decisions Made

- Business Analysis project approved.
- Current process will be documented.
- Stakeholder interviews will be scheduled.
- Existing KPIs will be collected.
- Solution options will not be discussed until current-state analysis is complete.
