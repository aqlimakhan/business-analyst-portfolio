# Business Problems Identified

## Sarah Thompson

- Manual compliance screening for all international orders
- No risk-based review process
- Analysts switch between multiple systems
- No SLA for escalated reviews
- Inconsistent compliance documentation

---

# Risks Identified

## Sarah Thompson

- Export volume growth may increase order backlogs.
- Inconsistent documentation may affect audit readiness.
- Manual reviews may contribute to analyst burnout.
- Shipment delays may impact customer satisfaction.

---

# Opportunities Identified

## Sarah Thompson

- Introduce a risk-based compliance review process.
- Improve analyst efficiency through workflow integration.
- Improve operational visibility.
- Standardize compliance documentation.
