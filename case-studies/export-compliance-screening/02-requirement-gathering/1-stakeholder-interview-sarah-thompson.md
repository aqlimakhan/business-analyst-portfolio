## Interview Notes
### Current Process

Sarah explained that the export compliance screening process begins when an international sales order is created in QAD ERP.

The system automatically flags all international orders for export compliance review.

A Trade Compliance Analyst performs the following activities:

- Screens the customer against denied party and sanctions lists.
- Reviews destination country restrictions.
- Determines whether an export license is required.
- Reviews previous screening history.
- Documents the screening results.
- Releases compliant orders in QAD ERP.

Orders requiring additional review are escalated to a Senior Trade Compliance Specialist.
