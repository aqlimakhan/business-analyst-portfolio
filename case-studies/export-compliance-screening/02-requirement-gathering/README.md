# Requirement Gathering

## Objective

The objective of this phase is to understand the current business process, identify stakeholder needs, document existing challenges, and establish a clear understanding of the business problem before defining requirements.

---

## Business Analysis Techniques Used

During this phase, the following BA techniques are applied:

- Stakeholder Analysis
- Stakeholder Interviews
- Process Discovery
- Document Analysis
- Active Listening
- Observation
- KPI Analysis
- Risk Identification

---

## Expected Outputs

By the end of this phase, the project team will have:

- A documented understanding of the current business process
- Identified business problems and pain points
- Current operational KPIs
- Business risks
- Stakeholder expectations
- Initial business opportunities

These findings will serve as the primary input into the Analysis phase.
